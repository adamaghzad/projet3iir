Game Overview

NieR:Automata is an action RPG developed by PlatinumGames and produced by Square Enix.

The story takes place in a distant future where humanity has retreated to the moon in the face of overwhelming forces from mechanical life forms.

Players take on the role of 2B, a member of the newly organized android squad "YoRHa", engaging in fierce battles for the survival of Earth.

More than just entertainment, experience a smooth 60fps open world that tells a story filled with various emotions.
